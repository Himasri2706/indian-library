Our Heritage

The Saraswati Digital Library is dedicated to preserving and sharing the vast ocean of Indian literature, philosophy, and history. Named after the goddess of knowledge, music, art, and wisdom, our mission is to make these timeless classics accessible to everyone, across all generations. From the Panchatantra tales designed to impart wisdom to children, to the profound spiritual inquiries of the Upanishads, we bridge the past and the future.
